Mango




Mangoes are rich in various vitamins and minerals, including:

Vitamin C: Boosts immunity, promotes skin health, and aids in collagen production. Vitamin A: Supports vision health and immune function. Vitamin E: An antioxidant that supports immune function and skin health. Vitamin K: Essential for bone health and blood clotting. Potassium: Helps regulate blood pressure and muscle function. Fiber: Aids digestion and promotes a feeling of fullness.
