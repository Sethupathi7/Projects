Strawberries are rich in various vitamins and minerals, including:

Vitamin C: Boosts immunity, promotes skin health, and acts as an antioxidant. Manganese: Supports bone health and helps regulate blood sugar. Folate (Vitamin B9): Essential for cell division and DNA synthesis. Potassium: Helps regulate blood pressure and maintain heart health. Vitamin K: Important for blood clotting and bone health. Fiber: Aids digestion and promotes a feeling of fullness.
