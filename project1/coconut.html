Coconut




Coconuts are rich in various vitamins, minerals, and other beneficial compounds, including:

Manganese: Supports bone health and metabolism. Copper: Essential for collagen production and iron absorption. Iron: Important for oxygen transport in the blood and energy production. Potassium: Helps regulate blood pressure and muscle function. Magnesium: Supports nerve function and muscle relaxation. Zinc: Supports immune function and wound healing.
